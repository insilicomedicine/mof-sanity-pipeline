This rule is commonly called **"Inbound = Outbound"**: the license under
which contributions are received is the same as the license under which the
project is distributed. No separate Contributor License Agreement (CLA) is
required.

You retain copyright over your contributions; you simply license them to the
project and its downstream users under MIT.

By opening a merge request you confirm that:

- The contribution is yours to license, or you have permission from the
